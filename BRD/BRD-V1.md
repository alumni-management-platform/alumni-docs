# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

Alumni Management Platform

## Version

1.0

## Objective

To build a centralized Alumni Management Platform that enables alumni associations to maintain accurate alumni records, manage alumni engagement, automate data collection, provide analytical insights, and establish a foundation for future AI-driven services.

---

## Current Challenges

### Data Fragmentation

Alumni information is scattered across:

* Excel files
* Google Sheets
* Personal records
* Paper documents

### Data Quality Issues

* Missing contact information
* Outdated employment details
* Duplicate records
* Inconsistent formats

### Limited Visibility

Association members cannot easily determine:

* Alumni locations
* Employment sectors
* Batch distributions
* Alumni engagement levels

### Manual Operations

Most activities are currently manual:

* Data collection
* Follow-up communications
* Reporting
* Profile updates

---

## Business Goals

### Goal 1

Establish a single source of truth for alumni information.

### Goal 2

Allow alumni to maintain their own profiles.

### Goal 3

Improve data completeness and accuracy.

### Goal 4

Provide actionable dashboards and reports.

### Goal 5

Reduce administrative effort through workflow automation.

---

## Stakeholders

### Primary

* Alumni
* Alumni Association Administrators

### Secondary

* Data Entry Operators
* Committee Members

---

## User Roles

### Alumni

Can:

* View own profile
* Request profile updates

### Data Administrator

Can:

* Create records
* Edit records
* Upload data
* Review changes

### Association Administrator

Can:

* Approve updates
* Access reports
* Manage users

### Super Administrator

Can:

* Configure system
* Manage roles
* Access all functions

---

## MVP Scope

### Authentication

* Secure login
* Role-based access

### Alumni Management

* Create alumni profile
* Update profile
* Search alumni
* View profile

### Bulk Upload

* Excel upload
* CSV upload
* Validation checks

### Approval Workflow

* Change requests
* Review process
* Approve / Reject

### Reporting

* Location reports
* Industry reports
* Batch reports
* Status reports

### Audit Trail

* Track all modifications
* Record approvals

## Success Metrics

* 90% alumni records digitized
* 80% profile completeness
* Search response under 3 seconds
* Bulk upload success rate above 95%
* Reduction in manual reporting effort

---

## Assumptions

* Internet access available
* Alumni have valid email addresses
* Historical data available in spreadsheet format

---

## Risks

### Poor Data Quality

Mitigation:
Validation and approval workflow.

### Low Alumni Participation

Mitigation:
Self-service portal and automated reminders.

### Data Privacy Concerns

Mitigation:
Role-based access and audit logging.
